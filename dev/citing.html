Citing Siphon¶

If Siphon played an important role in your research, then please add us to your reference list using one of the options below.

DOI: 10.5065/D6CN72NW

BibTeX entry¶

Example BibTeX entry:

@software{siphon,
  author = {May, Ryan and Arms, Sean and Leeman, John and Chastang, Julien },
  organization = {Unidata},
  title = {Siphon: A collection of {Python} Utilities for Accessing Remote Atmospheric and Oceanic Datasets},
  year = {2014 - 2017},
  version = {0.5.1},
  doi = {10.5065/D6CN72NW},
  url = {https://github.com/Unidata/siphon},
  address = {Boulder, Colorado}
}

AMS Journal Style¶

Example Citation:

May, R. M., Arms, S. C., Leeman, J. R., and Chastang, J., 2017:
    Siphon: A collection of Python Utilities for Accessing Remote Atmospheric
    and Oceanic Datasets. Unidata, Accessed 30 September 2017.
    [Available online at https://github.com/Unidata/siphon.]
    doi:10.5065/D6CN72NW.
